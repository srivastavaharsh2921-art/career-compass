Entrance Exam Pathways

Primary admission routes for 12th pass students:

Engineering & Architecture
JEE Main Jan & Apr

National-level exam for NITs, IIITs, and CFITs. Held in two sessions (usually January and April).

JEE Advanced May/June

Gateway to the IITs; open only to top JEE Main qualifiers. Held in May/June.

BITSAT May

For BITS Pilani campuses. Held in multiple sessions across May.

State CETs Apr/May

E.g., MHT-CET (Maharashtra), COMEDK (Karnataka), and KCET. Typically held in April/May.

NATA Apr - Jul

For B.Arch programs. Held in multiple tests from April to July.

Medical & Pharmacy
NEET UG May

The mandatory single national-level exam for MBBS, BDS, and AYUSH courses. Held in May.

ICAR AIEEA June/July

For B.Sc. Agriculture and allied courses. Usually held in June/July.

Law Pathways
CLAT December

Common Law Admission Test for 25 National Law Universities (NLUs). Held in December.

AILET December

For NLU Delhi. Held in December.

LSAT India January

Accepted by various top private law schools. Usually held in January.

General Degrees
CUET UG May/June

Central Universities Entrance Test is the primary gateway for admissions to central universities like Delhi University (DU), BHU, and JNU. Conducted in May/June.

Management
IPMAT May/June

Five-Year Integrated Program in Management at IIM Indore and IIM Rohtak. Held in May/June.

NPAT Apr - Jun

Entrance for NMIMS programs (BBA, B.Sc. Finance). Held between April and June.

SET May

Symbiosis Entrance Test for BBA and other undergraduate programs. Held in May.

Design & Fashion
NIFT Exam February

For B.Des and B.FTech at NIFT campuses. Held in February.

UCEED January

Conducted by IIT Bombay for B.Des. Held in January.

NID DAT Jan/Feb

Design Aptitude Test for NID. Held in January/February.

Defense Entry
NDA & NA Apr & Sep

National Defence Academy entry via UPSC. Exam is conducted twice a year, typically in April and September.

AI Guidance Tip

Science Stream students can also switch to Management (BBA), Commerce (B.Com), Law (BA LLB), or Design (B.Des) if they have matching creative/analytical interests. Commerce & Arts students cannot switch to B.Tech/MBBS due to board requirements.
